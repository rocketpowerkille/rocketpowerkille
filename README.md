### About Me & Current Focus

I am a 3rd Year Undergraduate at IIT Kanpur with a strong foundation in algorithmic problem-solving and systems architecture. My work bridges the gap between low-level performance optimization, quantitative finance, and artificial intelligence. I am deeply driven by the challenge of building autonomous agents and scalable systems that operate reliably in highly competitive or adversarial environments.

*   **Quantitative Finance & Game Theory:** I engineer high-frequency algorithmic trading engines and scalable event-driven limit-order-book backtesters. Recently, I developed a statistical arbitrage engine leveraging Engle-Granger cointegration and Particle Swarm Optimization to dynamically classify microstructural regimes. In the realm of game theory, I architected an autonomous poker agent utilizing Counterfactual Regret Minimization (CFR) and Monte Carlo heuristics, integrating a sub-millisecond combinatorial solver to compute exact river equities for all 990 deck combinations.
*   **AI/LLM Architectures & Security:** My focus lies in building stateful, production-grade LLM applications. As a Digital India Summer Intern at MeitY, I spearheaded a scalable AI/LLM Red Teaming platform for the India AI Mission, deploying an autonomous LLM-as-a-judge pipeline to proactively secure state-backed infrastructure against prompt injections and jailbreaks. I also engineer complex Agentic RAG frameworks (incorporating Self and Corrective RAG) using LangChain, Neo4j, and custom GraphRAG pipelines for relationship-driven semantic search.
*   **Web3 & Decentralized Finance:** I am actively building a production-grade Concentrated Liquidity Decentralized Exchange (CLMM) on Solana using Rust and the Anchor framework. This involves implementing highly precise Q64.96 fixed-point math and Geometric Price Ranges to achieve 4000x capital efficiency while preventing integer overflow risks. Additionally, I contribute to Bitcoin-related open-source projects as part of the Summer of Bitcoin 2026 cycle.
*   **Algorithmic Routing & Logistics:** I enjoy tackling complex pathfinding and optimization bottlenecks. Recently, I architected a centralized multi-agent drone dispatch algorithm that bypassed traditional A* limitations using a hybrid Floyd-Warshall and custom Dijkstra router with DSU-based station clustering, achieving 99.996% operational accuracy.
